What Is This?

A practical longevity research hub translating peer-reviewed science into actionable daily protocols. Rather than overwhelming you with research papers, this project distills the science into:

Body Composition Profiles

Protein targets, training priorities, and supplement stacks matched to whether you're lean, average, overweight, or obese.

Condition-Specific Protocols

Targeted interventions for diabetes, hypertension, inflammation, gut health, sleep issues, and more.

Biomarker Targets

Optimal ranges for key longevity markers with explanations of why each one matters.

How It Works

  1. Complete the intake form — Takes about 5 minutes. Covers your body composition, health conditions, medications, and lifestyle.
  2. Get your personalized protocol — Based on your profile, we recommend specific protein targets, supplement doses, exercise priorities, and timing.
  3. Track and iterate — Optimal protocols evolve as you improve. Re-check every 3-6 months or when key markers change.
Disclaimer: These protocols are for informational purposes only and are not medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider before making changes to your health regimen, especially if you have existing medical conditions or take medications.
